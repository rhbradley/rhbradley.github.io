About Me

Hello! I’m Robert Hampton Bradley, a Program Manager at Raytheon Technologies, based in Fullerton, CA, working remotely. With a strong foundation in engineering and business, I’m passionate about leading teams to deliver innovative solutions that make a real impact. I hold an MBA from Indiana University’s Kelley School of Business (2023, GPA 3.93), a Master’s in Mechanical Engineering (2019, GPA 4.00), and a Bachelor’s in Engineering Mechanics and Astronautics (2018, GPA 3.63), both from the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

In my current role, I manage the profit and loss for a highly technical radar program across various program lifecycles — Production, Sustainment, and Services. I oversee cross-functional teams to meet objectives on time and within budget. Strong execution on program deliverables have strengthened customer trust and has increased demand for our product both domestically and internationally. I thrive at the intersection of technical expertise and strategic leadership, and this site showcases my journey and skills.
